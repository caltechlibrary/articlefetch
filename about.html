articlefetch 0.0.1

working proof of concept

Authors

  • R. S. Doiel, https://orcid.org/0000-0003-0900-6903

A client that will find the person ID provided in Caltech Library's feed then retrieve the list of articles before fetching the PDFs from our RDM instance.
